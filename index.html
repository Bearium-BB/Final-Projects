About

Welcome to Druid Cove where the druids party all night and all day. The goal of this website is to help people play the druid class in Dungeons and Dragons. This website is supplementary help, you may find that you want more information and in that case I recommend you go to www.dndbeyond.com for complete coverage about classes, races, and monsters in D&D.

Hi, I'm Brett Bowley. I'm going to tell you a little about myself today, so let's get started. This is a final project that shows what we have learned from Mr. Hardman course. The website showcases all the different elements of web development that have been covered in the past year. If you enjoy the content, please let me know. Look to the footer of this website and you will be able to find a way to contact me or to help me update any new information has been added to D&D.
